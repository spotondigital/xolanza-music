About Us

Xolanza Music is an independent record label dedicated to nurturing and showcasing exceptional talent across genres like kwaito, afro pop, hip hop, and house music. Based in the vibrant township of Umlazi, south of Durban in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa.

Founded by Xolani Brian Nzama, a passionate music producer and songwriter, the label was created as a platform for his own music and to provide opportunities for aspiring artists who have been overlooked by the mainstream music industry.

At Xolanza Music, we believe in the power of music to uplift, inspire, and connect people, and we are committed to giving artists the tools and support they need to share their voices with the world.

If you're searching for fresh, innovative sounds and a diverse range of talented artists, you're in the right place. Explore the music, discover the stories, and join us on this exciting journey as we celebrate the richness of South African music and culture.

Vision

To revolutionize the music industry by breaking barriers, creating equitable opportunities, and empowering aspiring musicians worldwide. We are committed to fostering a culture of innovation, diversity, and positivity, establishing ourselves as the premier platform for launching successful careers in entertainment. Through collaboration and advocacy, we aim to drive lasting change, leaving a transformative legacy in South Africa, Africa, and beyond.

Mission

Our mission is to create an equitable and inclusive space where diverse artists can thrive. We provide recording facilities, artist development, and management services to empower 100+ emerging talents annually, helping them reach their full potential. Through music, we aim to amplify underrepresented voices, unite communities, and foster a more just and inclusive world.

Motto

The interpretation of the motto “We are music” is that it emphasizes the importance of freely expressing one’s artistic views, fostering creativity and individuality among the artists associated with Xolanza Music.

It suggests that the company values the unique perspectives and self-expression of its artists and encourages them to let their creativity flow in their work.

Meet Xolani “Bathi X” Nzama, a dynamic creative, seamlessly blending his roles as a label founder, music producer, and songwriter. His journey is a testament to his passion for music and his commitment to uplifting others in the industry. Through his independent record label, Xolanza Music. Xolani has created a platform dedicated to discovering, nurturing, and promoting talented artists across diverse genres.

Key Highlights of Xolani’s Career:

Founding Xolanza Music (2020):
In 2020, Xolani founded Xolanza Music, driven by a desire to do more than just create music. He wanted to empower other artists to achieve their dreams and share their talents with the world. As the Business Development Manager, Xolani oversees the label’s operations, including scouting and signing new talent, promoting their music, and guiding their career growth. His hands-on approach often sees him contributing creatively to songwriting and production

Music Releases


2020

Xolani, under his alter ego Luv D Nuz, released the kwaito single “Memories”, featuring Popzile (Snenhlanhla Mfeka), Truth LK (Qiniso Buthelezi), and Syanda Mculo (Siyanda Mthethwa). He also dropped the hip-hop single “B Ok” under the name Bathi X, featuring Klephent (Banele Ndlovu) and the late Sphelele Mavimbela (Mavi).

2023

Xolani released the afro-pop EP “Kasi Dream”, a project that showcased the talents of songwriters, producers, and vocalists from Umlazi. The lead single, “Thembisile”, became a standout track, highlighting his ability to blend local sounds with modern afro-pop influences.

2024

He released the afro single “Cloud 9”, featuring rising female vocalist Anga, further cementing his reputation as a hitmaker with an ear for fresh talent./p>

Current Projects


Xolani’s latest single, “Wena” (meaning “You” in Zulu), features Prince Olumor (Prince Gabela), a multi-talented producer, DJ, and vocalist. This track is the first single from Xolanza Music’s upcoming EP, “Matters Of The Heart”, set to be released later in the year.

The EP promises to explore themes of love, emotion, and personal growth through the lens of afro-house and contemporary sounds
